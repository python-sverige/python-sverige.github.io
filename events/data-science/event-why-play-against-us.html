Why play against us when you can play with us?: building collaborative human-AI gaming experiences with PyGame and reinforcement learning

Abstract: Lee Sedol’s five game match with Deep Mind’s AlphaGo ignited a renewed interest into the use of AI in gaming. From Kasparov to Sedol, from classic Atari games to immersive video games, humans have competed (and often been defeated) by computerised opponents. This talk will give an overview of how AI is used in gaming, the game development ecosystem in Python, how reinforcement learning can be used to train AI agents and how, instead of competing with AI based agents, we can design gaming experiences where human players and AI agents collaborate to complete levels.

About the speaker: Camilla Montonen is a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Elastic. She enjoys trail running, reading, gardening for bumblebees and stargazing.
