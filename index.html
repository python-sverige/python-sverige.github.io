We are super excited to come back together in person for this year's conference!

We know this is an odd request but we need your help. We run this organization on our spare time and we are just regular people doing regular things. In order to keep Pycon Sweden alive and energizing this amazing innovative European country, we kindly ask for your help in sponsorship.
